About Us

Tucked just outside the city of Leeds, Trissur Pooram is more than a South Indian restaurant – it is a heartfelt, family-run establishment and a 15-year culinary journey by its chef-owner. From humble beginnings in the vibrant city of Thrissur, to establishing a destination that draws food lovers from many parts of the country. This restaurant celebrates the rich traditions of Kerala through every carefully crafted dish.
